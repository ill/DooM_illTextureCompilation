Title                   : Gray textures v2

Filename                : txgray.zip (txgray.txt and txgray.wad)

Release Date		: April 24th, 2007

Author                  : Nick Baker, aka NiGHTMARE
		  
Email Address           : nick@frad.org

Description             : Over 600 variations of the gray textures (GRAY1, 			 	  GRAY2, etc). These include 84 different layouts, 			  	  with four colours of each (the standard gray, 			  	  plus brown, green, and tan), plus variations with
			  warning stripes and blue and red backgrounds.

			  In addition, several of the original gray textures 			  have been slightly modified.

Additional Credits To	: ID Software for the original textures (from
			  Doom 1 and Doom 2)

			  Espi, whose awesome Suspended In Dusk .wad
			  inspired several of the textures in this pack.
